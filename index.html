IJ Start Canon : Connect Canon Printer To Computer

IJ Start Canon

Set up your canon printer, and connect to a computer, smartphone or tablet via ij.start.canon. Checkout complete installation, Setup and Connection steps to start printing.

IJ Start Canon is a portal to download specific canon printer driver for your printer device to connect it with the computer or mobile or tablet etc. it comes along with the graphical interface to interact with your printer for various task to perform such as printing, scanning, saving files and picture, troubleshoot printer error and connecting printer wireless or using USB.

Download printer driver in your computer device

  1. Visit the IJ Start Canon website.
  2. Navigate to the support or downloads section.
  3. Locate your printer model.
  4. Download the driver software compatible with your operating system.
  5. Follow any on-screen instructions to complete the download and installation process.

Setup your Canon Printer Hardware

  1. Take the protective material off.
  2. Install the cartridges, or print heads.
  3. Pour the ink.
  4. After turning on the printer, confirm that the alarm lamp is off.
  5. Plug in the power cord and turn on the printer.

Connect your Printer to Computer Wireless

  1. Open the installed printer software.
  2. Select "Wireless LAN Connection" as the connection method.
  3. Follow the on-screen prompts to complete the setup.
  4. The software will automatically detect your printer on the network.
  5. Once setup is complete, print a test page from your computer to ensure the connection is successful.

Connect your Printer to Computer USB

  1. Open the installed printer software.
  2. Select "USB Connection" as the connection method.
  3. Follow the on-screen prompts to complete the setup.
  4. Once setup is complete, print a test page from your computer to verify that the printer is connected and functioning correctly.
